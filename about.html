About HyperCTF

The Game

HyperCTF is a capture-the-flag style cybersecurity competition run on the Facebook Capture The Flag (FBCTF) platform. HyperCTF consists of 175 challenges ranging from easy to hard. The easy challenges range from 0 to 100 points. The Medium challenges range from 100 to 200 points. The hard challenges range from 200 to 300 points. To win a HyperCTF game your team needs to get the most amount of points in the quickest amount of time.

The Team

The HyperCTF team consists of 3 high school students who wish to give aspiring cybersecurity engineers the tools necessary to be able to go into the field. To do this they have created HyperCTF.
